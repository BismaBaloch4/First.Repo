Biryani Recipe

Ingredient

  • 1 Cup Basmati Rice
  • 1 Pound Meat
  • 2 Medium Onions,chopped
  • 2 Cloves Garlic,minced
  • 1 TableSpoon Ginger Paste
  • 1 TeaSpoon Cumin Powder
  • 1 TeaSpoon Coriander Powder
  • 1/2 TeaSpoon Turmeric Powder
  • 1/2 TeaSpoon Red Chili Powder
  • Salt, to Taste
  • 2 TableSpoon Oil
  • 2 Cups Water
  • Fresh Cilantro,For Garnish

Instruction

  1. Rinse and soak the rice for 30 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add onions, garlic, and ginger paste.Cook until the onions are golden brown.Add the meat and cook until it's browned. Add cumin, Coriander, turmeric, and chili powder. Cook for 5 Minuts.
  3. In a large pot, layer the rice, meat mixture,and repeat.
  4. Add 2 cups of water, cover the pot, and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and fluffy.
  5. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve hot.
